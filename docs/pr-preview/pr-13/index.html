What is Didroom? ‚Üó

Customizable identity solution

Tailor Didroom to fit your unique identity management needs, ensuring seamless integration into your existing infrastructure.

EUDI-ARF Compliance

Didroom adheres to the European Union Digital Identity and Attribute (EUDI-ARF) framework, ensuring compliance with regulatory standards and data protection laws.

Security with Zenroom

Zenroom provides robust cryptographic security, safeguarding sensitive data and ensuring secure transactions.

Decentralization with Dyne.org DID

Dyne.org Decentralized Identifiers (DIDs) ensure decentralized and tamper-resistant identity management, enhancing privacy and control over personal data.
