Customizable Identity solution

Tailor DIDroom to fit your unique identity management needs, ensuring seamless integration into your existing infrastructure thanks to Zenroom and its ecosystem.

EUDI-ARF and more

DIDroom is compliant to the European Union Digital Identity and Attribute (EUDI-ARF) framework, it supports W3C-VC as well the zero knowledge proof schemes BBS and Coconut.

100% Open Source

The DIDroom Dashboard, the microservices and the mobile apps are fully open source, modular and extendable.

Decentralization with Dyne.org DID

Dyne.org Decentralized Identifiers (DIDs) ensure decentralized and tamper-resistant identity management, enhancing privacy and control over personal data.
